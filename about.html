Warps

Event Warps "Ripples in Reflection" & "Epochal Spectrum"

During the event, the drop rates of the limited 5-star character Black Swan (Nihility:Wind) and 4-star characters Misha (Destruction:Ice), Tingyun (Harmony:Lightning), and Guinaifen (Nihility:Fire will be boosted).
At the same time, the limited 5-star character Dan Heng • Imbibitor Lunae (Destruction:Imaginary) will return.

Event Warps "Brilliant Fixation" & "Bygone Reminiscence"

During the event, the drop rates of the limited 5-star Light Cone Reforged Remembrance (Nihility) and 4-star Light Cones Indelible Promise (Destruction), ResolutionShines As Pearls of Sweat (Nihility), and Dance! Dance! Dance! (Harmony) will be boosted for a limited time.
At the same time, the limited 5-star Light Cone Brighter Than the Sun (Destruction) will return.

Event Warps "Sparkling Splendor" & "Swirl of Heavenly Swirl"

During the event, the drop rates of the limited 5-star character Sparkle (Harmony: Quantum) and 4-star characters Sampo (Nihility: Wind), Qingque (Erudition: Quantum), and Hanya (Harmony: Physical) will be significantly boosted.
At the same time, the limited 5-star character Jing Yuan (Erudition: Lightning) will return.

Event Warps "Brilliant Fixation" & "Bygone Reminiscence"

During the event, the drop rates of the limited 5-star Light Cone Earthly Escapade (Harmony), the 4-star Light Cones Planetary Rendezvous (Harmony), Under the Blue Sky (Destruction), and Geniuses’ Repose (Erudition) will be boosted for a limited time.
At the same time, the limited 5-star Light Cone Before Dawn (Erudition) will return.

Events

Dreamchaser Bulletin

Event Duration: After the Version 2.0 update — 2024/03/25 03:59 (server time)

On your way to Penacony, you receive a tabloid in your inbox. It may be worth examining to see if there is any noteworthy news.

Explore Penacony and complete missions of defeating a certain number of enemies and others to obtain Stellar Jade ×500, Fuel, Traveler's Guide, Lost Crystal, Trace Material "Starfire Essence", and more

Hanu's Prison Break

Event Duration: 2024/02/08 12:00 — 2024/03/25 03:59 (server time)

Dr. Edward has created anew memory bubble with a unique theme, receiving rave reviews from customers who have experienced it. What makes it so exciting? You'll have to try it

Complete the stages "Bubble Pinball," "Dream Ticker,” “Hanu's Adventure" "Dreamwalker" to obtain Self-Modeling Resin ×1, Tracks of Destiny ×1, Stellar Jade ×1,020, Fateful Crossings: Hanu's Prison Break, and more.

Use Fateful Crossings: Hanu's Prison Break and choose one from [Sampo], [Asta], [Yukong], and [Guinaifen].

After this limited-time event ends, the "Hanu's Prison Break" event will become a part of the Conventional Memoir, and you can continue to experience its gameplay.

Dreamjolt TV

Event Duration: 2024/02/29 12:00 — 2024/03/25 03:59 (server time)

Some of the TV screens in Penacony start to air some strange programs, drawing the attention of you and a certain Penacony television station...

During the event, a total of 5 televisions will have their broadcast signals intercepted and will become available across 5 days. Defeat the mastermind behind the television signal interception to receive Tracks of Destiny ×1, Stellar Jade ×500, Relic Remains, Traveler's Guide, and other rewards.

Penacony Food Fest

Event Duration: 2024/03/06 12:00 — 2024/03/18 03:59 (server time)

The Clock Diner in the commercial district is holding an annual food festival. Bring your own ingredients and we'll cook them for free. Dreamy food, delivered by mail!

During the event, Trailblazers can complete Dreamy Cuisine by submitting the daily required meal ingredients and receive Stellar Jade ×500, Traveler's Guide, and consumables like Clockie Pizza (Single Slice) and Sliced Cake Stargazer.
